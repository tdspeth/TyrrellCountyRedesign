About

Tyrrell County

Discover Tyrrell County:

A Blend of Natural Beauty and Rich Heritage

Tyrrell offers a close-knit community atmosphere amidst its tranquil and unspoiled surroundings. The county prides itself on preserving its pristine environment, which includes parts of the Pocosin Lakes National Wildlife Refuge and the Palmetto-Peartree Preserve.

Tyrrell County is not just about picturesque landscapes; it's a community rooted in agriculture, education, and a growing tourism industry. Its commitment to sustainable development and conservation makes it a unique place to live, work, and visit. Here, history, community, and nature coalesce to create a living experience that is as enriching as it is peaceful.
